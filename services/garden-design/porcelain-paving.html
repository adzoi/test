Premium Porcelain Paving Installation

Porcelain paving is one of the most durable and low-maintenance options for patios and paths. It's frost-resistant, stain-resistant and won't fade — and with the right installation it looks sleek and modern while standing up to Irish weather.

At Leaf 2 Leaf we install outdoor porcelain tiles on a proper adhesive bed and joint with a flexible, weatherproof system. We use slip-resistant finishes suitable for outdoor use and offer a wide range of sizes and colours, including stone-effect and wood-effect looks.

⏱ Installation Time3–5 days (typical patio)
🛡️ DurabilityFrost, stain and fade resistant
🔧 MaintenanceVery low — occasional wash
🦶 Slip resistanceR11/R12 outdoor grades available
📐 SizesLarge format — 600×600, 800×800, 1200×600 and more
🎨 ColoursStone, wood, concrete and solid colours
📅 Lifespan25+ years with correct installation
