Creative Flower Bed Design & Installation

Flower beds and borders define your garden's character — whether you want bold colour, soft foliage or a low-maintenance scheme that looks good all year. Good design considers aspect, soil, height and flowering times so the bed works in your space.

At Leaf 2 Leaf we design and install flower beds from scratch: we prepare the soil, choose plants to suit your style and maintenance level, and plant so that the scheme establishes quickly and thrives.

⏱ Installation Time1–2 days (typical border)
🎨 StylesCottage, modern, tropical, low-maintenance
🔧 Maintenance levelsFrom low to high — we match your preference
📋 IncludesDesign, soil prep, plants, planting, mulch
